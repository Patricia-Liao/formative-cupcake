Our story

Some people saw a cupcake. We saw a lie. At Cup of Cake, we believe names should mean something. By taking cupcakes literally, we’ve created what we believe cupcakes were always supposed to be: Cake. In a cup. No wrappers. No confusion. No unnecessary structural risks. Just sponge, frosting, and toppings—layered exactly where they belong. Some call it dessert. We call it cupcake reform.
